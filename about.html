Meet the Founder

Jacob Wallace

Founder, Redeemed Digital

I’m Jacob Wallace — founder, builder, and lifelong systems thinker. Whether it’s in real estate, childcare, lending, or marketing, I’ve always been drawn to understanding how things work and how they can work better. That curiosity, paired with a passion for helping others, led me to create Redeemed Digital — a company built on faith, strategy, and purpose.

My career has been rooted in relationship-building through sales and marketing. I’ve seen firsthand how presenting yourself professionally opens doors and builds trust — and your business should be no different. As small business owners, we often wear all the hats. We’re busy running day-to-day operations, managing people, solving problems — all while trying to grow. That’s where I come in. My goal is to help you showcase your business in the way it truly deserves — with modern websites, streamlined digital tools, and smart automation systems that do the heavy lifting in the background.

At the core of Redeemed Digital is a commitment to purpose — rooted in 1 Corinthians 10:31:
“So whether you eat or drink or whatever you do, do it all for the glory of God.”
This verse guides everything I do. I believe excellence in business is a form of worship — and that doing our best, in every detail, is one way we glorify God.

I live in Grove, Oklahoma, with my wife, Kaylee, and our three amazing children. We’re a family rooted in faith, community, and creativity. Kaylee runs a local dance studio and childcare center, and together we stay busy juggling family life and entrepreneurship. Whether we’re cheering on our kids, building businesses, or serving in our community, we believe in doing everything with purpose — and Redeemed Digital is a reflection of that.
